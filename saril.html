About Us

Helen’s Store started in 2018. Since the market was quite far from our area, Helen decided to open a small store that sells everyday kitchen essentials such as condiments and other household needs. The store was built to help our neighborhood conveniently buy their daily cooking ingredients without having to travel far to the market. Over time, Helen’s Store became a trusted place in the community for affordable and accessible kitchen supplies.
